<design_system>
**Greptile-Inspired Design Guidelines:**

1. **Color Palette:**
   - Background: Warm cream/beige (#f5f0e8 or oklch(0.96 0.02 85)) instead of pure white
   - Cards: Pure white (#ffffff) for contrast against warm background
   - Primary: Keep existing emerald green
   - Text: Dark gray for headings, muted for body

2. **Typography:**
   - Large, bold headings (text-3xl to text-5xl, font-bold)
   - Generous line height and letter spacing
   - Clear hierarchy between heading, subheading, body

3. **Spacing:**
   - Generous padding (p-8 to p-12 for sections)
   - Large gaps between elements (gap-6 to gap-8)
   - max-w-4xl containers for comfortable reading width

4. **Components:**
   - White cards with subtle shadow (shadow-sm) on warm background
   - Rounded corners (rounded-xl for cards)
   - Section labels in brackets "[PLAYER SELECTION]" style
   - Buttons with rounded-full or rounded-lg

5. **Visual Accents:**
   - Subtle borders (border-border/50)
   - Clean, minimal aesthetic
   - Lots of whitespace
</design_system>
